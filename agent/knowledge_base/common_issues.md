# Common IT Issues

## VPN Connection Troubleshooting
1. Open your VPN client and ensure your credentials are correct.
2. Verify that your internet connection is active.
3. Restart the VPN service from the settings menu.
4. If the issue persists, collect log files for escalation.

## Email Configuration (Outlook)
1. Open Outlook and navigate to **File > Account Settings**.
2. Verify that the incoming and outgoing servers match your IT policy.
3. Run the built-in Outlook repair tool.
4. Restart Outlook and confirm the inbox syncs.
